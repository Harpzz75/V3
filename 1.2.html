I will use an eCommerce website as an example, First of all there should be some kind of Project Management System considered. Wether this is you yourself or a dedicated person or team to do this.

Things to be considered: What is the purpose of the website? Obviously to sell things but to whom, a targeted audience? What platform will be hosted on? Very important to get this established early on. Considering budget, purpose etc. Development of the site is very important, it needs to be properly synchronised so it works seamlessly across all devices/browsers etc. Developers will need to work together in unison and be actively communicating with the business owners directly or through a Project Manager.

A B2B website may need more back end work while a B2C site could involve more front end development as to improve a customers shopping experience. The design or look and feel of this sight is also very important, as this will be the online presence of the company as a whole.

So to recap, Oranisational skills (time and people management) IT skills, a developer(s) will be need to build the site and to maintain it whist it is operational. Design skills, a designer of some description will be needed to create graphics fro the pages front end. Also a general understanding of the whole eCommerce industry as to be able to keep up with current trends and internet developments and advances.
