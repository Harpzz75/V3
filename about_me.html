I have always been interested in art and design, from early school days to the present. Fortunately I have been able to gain some qualifications and work in the industry for many years. More recently with the rapid advances in technology, especially web technology, I have swung to a more digital
design way of thinking and working and have been involved in many fantastic
projects including web banners/ads/animations, email template design and builds, UI/UX design, Social Media
ads/graphics/campaigns etc and lots of other exiting web graphics and projects.

o with having a good understanding of overall graphic design processes and techniques I find myself wanting to know and learn more about “The Dark Side”, as it is sometimes referred to by Graphic Designers, meaning the back end functioning of web
pages/apps/banners/ads and the like. Having worked closely with developers for many years I have a developed
a fascination and keenest to explore this more.

I love keeping up with the ever changing world which this is, I find it both challenging and thrilling as there is always something new to learn and discover. Not a day goes by when you don’t find a new solution to a question or you learn something new. And the possibilities are become increasingly more
endless as the technology grows and improves.

So this why I joined Code Nation, to try and realise my ambitions and put them into practice. My experience so far has been awesome, I have really enjoyed all of it, the course so far has included many different practices from organisational skills, team management, time management, logical thinking, a bit of
maths/equations/formulas etc and above all giving us an idea as to what the whole industry sector is all about.
