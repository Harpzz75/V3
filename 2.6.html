Copyright issues can usually occur when a person or business uses someone else's content as their own. Either by not purchasing said content properly or not accrediting the contents creators properly. Copyright issues can relate to text, images, videos or another unauthorised use of third party content. Many royalty free options are available now for either a small fee or some kind of accreditation. Sites such as iStock, Shutterstock, AdobeStock offer subscriptions where content can be used breaking any rules. Other sites such as Pexels offer completely free content which can be used for personal or commercial use.

Having been a designer for many years I fully understand the importance of creating content across all medias while considering where the content comes from, if it has not been created by myself.
