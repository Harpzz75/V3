"Trello is an excellent option for individuals or teams to to organise and manage their digital projects using Trello app. It is based on the KANBAN system and is an excellent organisational system. Basically a list of "To Dos", "Doings", and "Dones". So being aware, in a real time environment, of what you need to do, what you are doing right now and what you have done. Teams could assign a different role to each member to complete during the project and each can edit/update the Trello board as they go, so that the other team members can see where the project is up to anytime and anywhere.


GitHub is a code hosting platform, teams of developers and designers can work together on projects from anywhere at anytime. So for exmple a team of 6 developers could be amending different pages and can then upload to GitHub to update the master file. The compination of Trello and GitHub to organise digital projects is a dream combination, helping people to streamline their workflow and be more efficient.
